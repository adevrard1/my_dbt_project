This dashboard tracks overall sales performance by monitoring key metrics at a monthly level such as:
- total_sales_amount
- evol_sales_amount_vs_precedent_month_in%
- evol_sales_amount_vs_lastyear_in% 
- total_sales_quantity
- evol_sales_quantity_vs_precedent_month_in%
- evol_sales_quantity_vs_lastyear_in% 
- nb_orders
- evol_sales_nb_orders_vs_precedent_month_in%
- evol_sales_nb_orders_vs_lastyear_in% 
- avg_order_value
- evol_avg_order_value_vs_precedent_month_in%
- evol_avg_order_value_vs_lastyear_in%

vs_precedent_month calculates month-over-month growth.
vs_precedent_month calculates year-over-year growth.
