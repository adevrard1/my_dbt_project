{% docs mrt_products_performance %}

This table aggregates monthly product performance by combining sales, pricing, and inventory data. 
It provides key metrics such as : 
- revenue
- contribution to monthly revenue
- quantity sold
- average price
- applied discounts

It also includes an estimate of the number of days of inventory remaining based on the last 30 days of sales, along with a stock management status (out of stock, understocked, overstocked, optimal level) to support commercial and inventory decision-making.
